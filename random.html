The art of random sampling is one of the most useful tools to an analyst who is trying to make sense of something that is far greater than what he can sense. It allows the analyst to understand and describe the behavior and processes governing a macro system be it disease in mammals or purity of metals coming out of a processing plant, when the bigger picture is too big to fit into a sheet of paper it is better to cut it up into smaller puzzle pieces and randomly select a few pieces and use it to guess the larger picture.
